🌟 DL-based AEM data analysis system

Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) remains a serious health challenge worldwide. One of the major problems in the treatment of liver cancer is that it is prone to frequent recurrence after curative resection. My current focus is on multi-omics analysis of primary and recurrent tumors to explore key factors in the recurrence and metastasis mechanisms of liver cancer.

Multi-omics

this current study can be a valuable resource yielding important insights into the evolutionary dynamics of metastatic progression in HCC and providing a rational foundation for developing predictive biomarkers and therapeutic targets for late-stage HCC patients.
